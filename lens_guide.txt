**PHASE 1: DISCOVERY**
*Extract raw data and identify human friction.*
* **The Signal Extractor:** Translates raw ramblings into business kernels.
* **User Advocate:** Maps emotional needs and hidden friction.

**PHASE 2: DEFINITION**
*Structure the product and determine feasibility.*
* **User Experience Architect:** IA/IxD, and cognitive load.
* **Product Manager:** MVP and JTBD prioritization.
* **Technical Lead:** Feasibility and scaling.

**PHASE 3: STRESS-TESTING & RECONSTRUCTION**
*Break the logic before the market does, then rebuild it.*
* **The Devil’s Advocate:** Fatal flaws via logic/market failure.
* **The Solutions Architect:** Rebuilds broken ideas into viable frameworks.
* **Business Strategist:** ROI and competitive moats.

**PHASE 4: DISTRIBUTION**
*Get it to the user.*
* **Growth Marketer:** Distribution and Acquisition logic.
